Detailed Description

(package-private)
This POJO contains the data from a card obtained in response to a card selection request.

These data are the selection status and the responses, if any, to the additional APDUs sent to the card CardResponseAdapter).

See also
calypsonet::terminal::card::spi::CardSelectionRequestSpi
